## English Version

Dear team, good afternoon.

IC Kaohsiung is launching the new "**F&B Culinary Journey Map**" — customers earn dice rolls after dining at any of our 5 restaurants, walk the Monopoly map, draw lottery prizes, and win F&B credit / drink vouchers / room nights.

▶ **How customers play**
- Scan the table-side QR code with the LINE built-in camera
- LINE auto-adds us as a friend + auto-enters the game + auto-binds your outlet (zero typing)
- Spend NT$2,000+ at checkout → self-redeem by scanning the e-invoice or photographing the POS slip → earn dice rolls
- Walk the Monopoly map → draw lottery / claim fixed rewards
- Hit 15 points → grand prize → auto-progress to the next season

▶ **When staff intervene (90% no-op most of the time)**
- Customer asks how to play → walk them to the table standee and demo verbally
- Customer is stuck (sees the yellow "Please see staff" banner) → use the "**Checkout QR**" tool to issue a QR
- Customer disputes an invoice → use the "**Customer Lookup**" tool and show them the system record

**Key rules**
- Every NT$2,000 spent = 1 dice roll, capped at 5
- Hit 15 points → grand prize, then a new season starts automatically
- The staff operations PIN is given verbally by your F&B manager — never write it on a wall
- Detailed step-by-step instructions are in the attached PPT — 20 bilingual slides covering the three critical URLs + PIN, how to save them to your phone home screen, complete Checkout QR / Customer Lookup tutorials, all five restaurant QR codes, the error-message decoder, four role-play scenarios, and a quick reference card.
