### English Version

Dear team,

IC Kaohsiung is launching the new **F&B Culinary Journey · Monopoly Game** — customers earn dice rolls after dining at any of our 5 restaurants, walk the Monopoly map, and win F&B credit, drink vouchers, or room nights. **Demo Day: 2026-05-31**.

**Quick Overview**

▶ **How customers play**
1. Scan the table-side QR with the LINE built-in camera
2. LINE auto-adds IC as a friend + auto-enters game + auto-binds the outlet (zero typing)
3. Spend NT$2,000+ at checkout → self-redeem by scanning e-invoice or photographing the POS slip
4. Walk the Monopoly map → draw lottery / claim fixed rewards
5. Hit 15 points → grand prize → auto-progress to next season

▶ **When staff intervene (90% no-op)**
1. Customer asks *how to play* → walk them to a standee and demo verbally
2. Customer is **stuck** (sees the yellow "see staff" banner) → use the **Checkout QR** tool to issue a QR
3. Customer **disputes** an invoice → use the **Customer Lookup** tool and show the system record

**Key Rules**
- Every NT$2,000 spent = 1 dice roll, capped at 5
- 15 points to grand prize, then a new season starts automatically
- The staff PIN will be given **verbally** by your F&B manager — never post it on a wall

**Full instructions are in the attached PPT** — 20 bilingual slides covering:
- The three critical URLs + PIN at a glance
- How to save URLs to your phone home screen (iOS + Android, 4 steps each)
- Checkout QR · 6-step workflow (with mock-up screens)
- Customer Lookup · 3-color result tutorial
- Per-outlet QR codes for all 5 restaurants
- Error-message decoder table
- Anti-fraud rules + 5 hard prohibitions
- 4 role-play scenarios for manager-led practice drills
- Quick reference card (print and pin to the service station drawer)

**Please review the attached deck thoroughly before 2026-05-30.** Your outlet manager will schedule practice sessions.

Questions? Reach me at `tony.chen6@ihg.com` or your F&B department head.
